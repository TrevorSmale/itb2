## Creating the Cork (15 minutes)

1. **Extruding**  
   - **Shortcut Method:**  
     - In `Edit Mode`, <kbd>E</kbd> to extrude.  
   - **GUI Method:**  
     - `Mesh > Extrude > Extrude Region`.

2. **Loop Cuts**  
   - **Shortcut Method:**  
     - <kbd>Ctrl+R</kbd> for a loop cut, scroll to adjust the n
