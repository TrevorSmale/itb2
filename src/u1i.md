## Creating our string

1. **Adding Curve**  
   - **Shortcut Method:**  
     - In `Object Mode`, <kbd>Shift+A</kbd> curve > nurbs curve.  
   - **GUI Method:**  
     - `add > curve > nurbs curve`

2. **Adjusting Curve**  
   - **Shortcut Method:**  
     - In `Edit Mode`, <kbd>1</kbd> 
     - Select and <kbd>g</kbd> to grab and move points
     - extrude with <kbd>e</kbd>

3. **Thickenning the curve**  
   - **Shortcut Method:**  
     - In `data` tab of the info panel
     - We click a dropdown for `geometry`
     - We adjust the `depth` slider
